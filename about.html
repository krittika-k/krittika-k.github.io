Hi, I’m Krittika (কৃত্তিকা ), a PhD student at ____.

I grew up in Assam, India, a state known for its world-famous Assam tea (the best, of course! 😉), lush rice fields, exquisite Muga silk, rich oil reserves, and the mighty Brahmaputra River, which shapes our land and culture. I deeply value my heritage and the land that nurtured me. As one of our finest musicians, Dr. Bhupen Hazarika, beautifully said:

অসম আমাৰ ৰূপহী গুণৰো নাই শেষ ("Our Assam is beautiful, her qualities are endless.")

When I’m not pulling my hair studying stats and coding, I’m probably:

  • Experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen (Ask me about Assamese cuisine!)
  • Taking long walks, especially by the lake
  • Reading fiction
  • Gardening—because plants makes me very happy
  • Watching endless dog videos and missing my own dog back home... a lot. (Yes, I will absolutely show you cutie pictures of my doggo!)
